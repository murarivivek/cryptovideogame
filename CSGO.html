FAQ

Q. How to Buy?
A. You need to use Metamask to buy Games.

Q. What is the price increase rate?
A. Everytime you buy a token, the price of the token increases by 2X. When the item is sold:

  • Current Owner of the Game gets 10%
  • Contract creator gets 10%
  • Owner of the Item/token gets 80%

So, current owner gains 60% profit when someone buys from them.

Q. Where do you deliver the game items?
A. We don't deliver any game items. Think of this like Monopoly where you buy game items instead of cities. You get returns when someone else buys the game items from you.

Q. I have a question
A. Please reach out to us on our Discord. We are always available to answer your questions.

Q. What is the future of this game?
A. We plan to introduce more features to provide dividends for the existing players and owners. The game would be extended to generate profits not only to the players who sold their tokens but also to the existing holders
