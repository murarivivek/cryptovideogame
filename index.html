FAQ

Q. How to Buy?
A. You need to use Metamask to buy Games.

Q. Do you give dividends?
A. Whenever anyone buys a Game Item from a game room, Game owners get dividends of 10%. In future, we will also introduce Game Mods which give Game Owners and Game Item owners more dividends.

Q. What is the price increase rate?
A. Everytime you buy a Game, the price of the token increases by 2X. When the Game is sold:

  • Current Owner of the Game gets 90%
  • Contract creator gets 10%

Everytime you buy a Game Item (Weapons, characters, maps etc), the price of the token increases by 2X. When the Game Item is sold:

  • Owner of the Game Item/token gets 80%
  • Owner of the Game gets 10%
  • Contract creator gets 10%

In future, we plan to introduce dividends for Game Items where anytime someone buys Game Mods, Game Item owners and Game owners are given dividends

Q. Where do you deliver the games?
A. We don't deliver any games. Think of this like Monopoly where you buy games instead of cities. You get returns when someone else buys the games from you.

Q. I have a question
A. Please reach out to us on our Discord. We are always available to answer your questions.

Q. What is the future of this game?
A. Based on the response, we plan to develop the game play to include more features.
