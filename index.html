FAQ

Q. How to Buy?
A. You need to use Metamask to buy Games.

Q. What is the price increase rate?
A. Everytime you buy a token, the price of the token increases by 2X. So, current owner gains 100% profit when someone buys from them.

Q. Where do you deliver the games?
A. We don't deliver any games. Think of this like Monopoly where you buy games instead of cities. You get returns when someone else buys the games from you.

Q. I have a question
A. Please reach out to us on our Discord. We are always available to answer your questions.

Q. What is the future of this game?
A. Based on the response, we plan to develop the game play to include more features.
